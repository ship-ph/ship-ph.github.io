Singa Ship Management Phils., Inc. (SSMPI)

SSMPI is a Cruise Ship Personnel Specialist that deploys hotel, deck and engine crew to major cruise ship companies worldwide such as Cunard Line, Seabourn Cruise Lines, SeaDream Yacht Club, Windstar Cruises and The World Residences at Sea. SHIP serves as the training arm of SSMPI for its entry level personnel for hotel positions and as a test cooking and culinary training venue for its principals. SHIP also conducts the Marlins Test of Spoken English (TOSE), Spoken English Assessment for Cruise Ship Staff (SPEACS) and the Computer – based English Test for Seafarers.

Graduates of SHIP training courses with the following minimum requirements can be referred to SSMPI for possible shipboard deployment. 22-32 years old

  • At least 5’6” for Male and 5’2” for Female
  • With at least 1 year College Level
  • With a minimum of 1 year and 6 months recent; related hotel / restaurant / shipboard experience
  • With good communication skills
  • With complete requirements – passport, seaman’s book, Seafarer’s Registration Certificate (SRC), NBI clearance, Basic Training, Crowd Management Training, Birth Certificate, CAV, Diploma or Transcript of Records, Training Certificate, Employment Certificate, SSS Number, TIN Number, PHILHEALTH Number and PAGIBIG Number.
